Lasagna

Description

A tasty dish for all families. This italian dish is beloved by many for it's relative ease of creation and ability to fill many empty stomachs.

This is a general recipe, but I always encourage experimenting with your own style of cooking.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it rest
