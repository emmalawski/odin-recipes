Chili

Description

This delicious chili is easy to make and sure to be a crowd pleaser. Great for potlucks, family gatherings, and even just a friday dinner.

As always, I recommend playing around with seasonings and such. Cooking is about finding your own ways to create delicious dishes, and creativity is always encouraged.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato products
  • Seasonings
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Peppers
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the beef
  2. Chop your onion and pepper, and mince your garlic
  3. Drain your beef
  4. Mix your beef, veggies, tomatoes, and seasonings in your slow cooker
  5. Cook on high for 3 hours or low for 6 hours
  6. Serve with some shredded cheese on top
