Grilled Cheese

Description

A simple meal for when you don't have the time for a full dish, but want to skip fast food.

This is a favorite of college students, but it still hits the spot for anyone.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread
  2. Put the slice of bread butter side down in a hot pan
  3. Place 2 slices of cheese on that bread and place the other slice on that with the butter side up
  4. Flip sandwich
  5. When the cheese is fully melted, remove from pan and enjoy
